Fellowship

We are connecting the next generation of makers, designers, and engineers to top ASEAN NGOs and social enterprises. Fellows gain access to a world-class global network of technologists and social enterprises as well as support from their peers in the fellowship class. Nonprofits and social enterprises come to us with pressing projects, and fellows are organized into teams and project managers accoridng to geographic region to tackle these projects.

Exposure

Many efforts go unnoticed. Through our blog, we feature the social enterpreneurs and nonprofits who are trailblazing efforts to tackle tough social problems, focusing in Thailand and the ASEAN countries.
