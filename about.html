USING DATA TO SCALE IMPACT

Our goal is to close the technical talent gap worldwide.

Through our experience in both the technology and social development fields, we see social organizations lacking in technical talent that is so readily accessible in other fields. CodeCreate is our answer to this. We do this in a three prong approach: a fellowship and data consultancy that connects social enterprsies with CodeCreate "fellows" who go through our application process, DataMeasure, a database to connect social enterprises with volunteers, and a social return on investment initiative. We get rid of the barriers such as language and technical barriers as the middleman in the process for international social enterprises.
